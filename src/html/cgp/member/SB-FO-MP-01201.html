Delete My Account

This is a guide to cancel membership.
Be sure to read the guidelines before applying for membership cancellation.
Nickname cannot be reused or recovered
The nickname used at the time of membership withdrawal cannot be reused or restored.
Post cannot be deleted
After withdrawal, the member information is deleted and the identity cannot be verified, so the post cannot be deleted. Please delete any unnecessary posts before withdrawal.
Delete personalized services
All personal service records such as CLUB, forums, and games are deleted, and deleted data cannot be recovered. If you need a backup, please make a backup before leaving.
Deletion of goods and services
All points and goods used on the website will be deleted upon withdrawal. Be sure to use all of them before withdrawal.
  • Membership withdrawal is completed 30 days after the date of application for withdrawal.
  • It is possible to cancel the membership withdrawal request within 30 days after the membership withdrawal request.
