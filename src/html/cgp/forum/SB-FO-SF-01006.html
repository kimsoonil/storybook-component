The group TWICE is drawing attention as it is nominated for the American Music Awards (2022 American Music Awards, hereinafter referred to as “AMA”), one of the top three music awards in the United States.

According to the list of award nominees announced by 2022 AMA on its official website and SNS on the 13th (local time), TWICE was named in the “Favorite K-Pop Artist” category, which was newly established this year.”AMA” is a prestigious award ceremony called the top three U.S. music awards along with the “Grammy Awards” and the “Billboard Music Awards,” and TWICE was nominated for the first time at the awards ceremony.

He was also nominated for the “Best K-Pop” category of the “2022 MTV European Music Awards,” which was announced earlier on the 12th.TWICE proved its global popularity again by being listed in two of the top three music awards ceremonies in the U.S., “AMA,” and Europe’s largest music awards ceremony “MTV EMA.”

TWICE’s 11th mini-album “BETWEEN 1&2” (Beatwin One&2) released in August this year recently surpassed 1 million cumulative shipments of Circle Chart (formerly Gaon Chart) and achieved million sales.The album entered third place on the U.S. Billboard 200 and ranked 129th on the latest chart for the sixth consecutive week.

Meanwhile, TWICE will hold a fan meeting “TWICE FANMEETING ONCE HALLOWEEN 3” at KBS Arena in Gangseo-gu, Seoul on November 5 at 1 p.m. and 7 p.m.Pre-bookings were held for the 3rd fan club ONCE (fandom name: ONCE) on Oct. 7 and 11, and general reservations were held on the 12th, and tickets were sold out after opening general reservations.

TWICE will simultaneously hold a fan meeting at 7 p.m. on the Beyond LIVE platform and make special memories with more fans.The online ticket will be available on Beyond LIVE’s official website from 3 p.m. on the 17th.
